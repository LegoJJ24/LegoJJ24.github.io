Muddy Buddies (Puppy Chow)

Description

The BEST Muddy Buddies recipe! Also known as "puppy chow", this classic treat is made with chocolate, peanut butter, Chex cereal, and powdered sugar. It’s a crowd-pleasing, no-bake snack that’s ready in 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 9 cups Rice Chex cereal
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Add Chex cereal to a large bowl.
  2. Add chocolate chips, peanut butter, and butter to a medium microwave-safe bowl.
  3. Microwave for 1 minute. Stir well. Continue to microwave in 30-second increments, stirring between, until smooth.
  4. Stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Pour chocolate mixture over the Chex cereal and stir gently until all the cereal is evenly coated.
  6. Pour half of the cereal mixture into a large Ziplock bag. Add half of the powdered sugar (about 3/4 cup). Seal the bag and shake well to coat.
  7. Pour onto a large baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Repeat with the remaining cereal and powdered sugar.
  9. Allow to cool completely before storing in an airtight container.
