Muddy Buddies (Puppy Chow)

A very easy and quiet delicious snack to make

This is a classic and popular party snack made with Chex cereal coated in a delicious mixture of chocolate, peanut butter, and powdered sugar.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups Chex cereal (Rice, Corn, or a combination)
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 4 Tbsp (1/2 stick) salted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Measure cereal into a very large mixing bowl. Set aside.
  2. Place 1 cup of powdered sugar into two separate large ziplock bags (1 cup in each). Set aside.
  3. In a medium saucepan, combine chocolate chips, peanut butter, and butter.
  4. Cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture is melted and smooth.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Pour the chocolate mixture over the cereal in the bowl. Stir gently with a spatula until all cereal is evenly coated. Allow to cool for a few minutes.
  7. Divide the coated cereal evenly between the two bags of powdered sugar. Seal the bags.
  8. Shake each bag until the cereal is thoroughly coated. Spread on wax paper to cool completely.
