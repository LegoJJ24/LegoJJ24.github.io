Easy French Toast

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (The secret ingredient for fluffiness!)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8 thick slices of bread (Texas Toast or Brioche work best)

Instructions

  1. Heat a griddle or skillet to medium heat (350Â°F) and grease generously with butter.
  2. In a blender (or shallow dish), combine eggs, milk, flour, sugar, salt, cinnamon, and vanilla. Blend or whisk until smooth.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the mixture, ensuring both sides are coated but not soaking for too long.
  4. Place bread on the hot griddle. Cook for a few minutes on each side until golden brown.
  5. Serve warm with syrup and fresh fruit.
