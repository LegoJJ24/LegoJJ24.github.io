Buttermilk Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 Tablespoons melted butter
  • 2 Tablespoons milk (optional, to thin batter)

Instructions

  1. Heat a griddle or large skillet to medium heat (about 350°F). Grease with butter.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the buttermilk, beaten eggs, and melted butter. Mix gently until just combined—do not overmix! It is okay if there are lumps.
  4. If the batter is too thick, add the milk a tablespoon at a time.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the hot griddle for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface and pop (about 2-3 minutes), then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
