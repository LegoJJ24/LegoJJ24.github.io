Belgian Waffles

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs, separated
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil (or melted coconut oil)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your waffle iron.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Separate the egg yolks and whites into two different bowls.
  4. In the bowl with the yolks, add the milk, oil, and vanilla. Whisk to combine.
  5. Pour the wet yolk mixture into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  6. Beat the egg whites with a hand mixer until stiff peaks form. Gently fold the egg whites into the batter.
  7. Pour batter into the hot waffle iron and cook according to your machine's instructions until golden and crisp.
