Homemade Chicken Pot Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup celery, sliced
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt & 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon celery seed & 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon paste
  • 1 cup milk
  • 8 ounces frozen mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 unbaked pie crusts

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Place chicken in a pot, cover with water, and boil for 10-15 minutes until cooked. Chop into bite-size pieces. Reserve 1 ¾ cups of the cooking water.
  3. In a large saucepan, cook onions and celery in butter until soft. Stir in flour, salt, pepper, celery seed, garlic powder, and bouillon.
  4. Gradually stir in milk and the reserved chicken water. Simmer until thickened.
  5. Stir in the chopped chicken and frozen vegetables. Remove from heat.
  6. Place one pie crust in a 9-inch pie pan. Pour in the filling. Top with the second crust, seal the edges, and cut slits in the top.
  7. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes until crust is golden brown.
