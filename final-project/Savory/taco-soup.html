Easy Taco Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained
  • 1 can (17 oz) corn, drained
  • 1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or chili powder & cumin)

Instructions

  1. In a large soup pot, brown the ground beef and onion over medium heat until meat is cooked through. Drain grease.
  2. Add the beans, corn, tomato sauce, beef broth, and taco seasoning to the pot.
  3. Stir well to combine.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Serve warm with tortilla chips, sour cream, and shredded cheese.

Source: Tastes Better From Scratch
